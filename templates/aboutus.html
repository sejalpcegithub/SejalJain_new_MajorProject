About Summer Intenship 2022

Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Poornima Institue of Engineering and Technology (PIET) offered 45 days Summer Intenship on Data Science and Machine Learning for 2nd year students which provided us the enviornment to build up ideas, do research and development. PIET have been accredited by National Board of Accredation(NBA) in year 2017-2018. PIET had achieved incredible milestones because of their team work and innovative ideas.
